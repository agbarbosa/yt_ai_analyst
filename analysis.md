# 90-Day Channel Optimization Strategy for "Mulheres em Deus" 

## Recommendation 1: Content Structure 

### Priority: Critical 

#### Current Issue: The channel has a high percentage of Shorts (75%), but lacks mid-form content which could improve viewer retention and engagement. 

### Specific Action Items: 
1. **Develop a series of mid-form videos (3-8 minutes)** focusing on popular topics like "Jejum Florescer" and "Caixinha de Conselho". 
- Effort: High 
- Timeline: 1 week per video 
2. **Analyze top-performing Shorts** to determine themes or topics that can be expanded into mid-form content. 
- Effort: Medium 
- Timeline: 2 days 
3. **Create a content calendar** to plan and schedule these videos. 
- Effort: Low 
- Timeline: 1 day 

#### Expected Impact: 
- Metric: Watch Time & Retention 
- Current Value: 51.84% 
- Projected Value: 55% 
- Percentage Improvement: ~6% 
- Timeframe: 90 days 
- Confidence Level: High 
#### Reasoning: Mid-form content typically holds viewers' attention longer than Shorts, contributing to higher retention rates which are heavily weighted in the algorithm. 
#### Success Metrics: 
- Track Average View Duration (AVD) and Average Percentage Viewed (APV) for new mid-form content. 
- Compare retention rates before and after the implementation.

- Projected Value: 7% 
- Percentage Improvement: ~25% 
- Timeframe: 90 days

## Recommendation 4: Thumbnail Improvement 
### Priority: Medium 
#### Current Issue: While CTR is above benchmark, there is potential to optimize thumbnails further to attract more viewers, especially given the importance of first impressions. 
#### Specific Action Items:

- Effort: Medium 
- Timeline: Each test runs for 2 weeks 
2. **Use high-contrast colors and clear, readable text** in thumbnails. 
- Effort: Low 
- Timeline: 30 minutes per thumbnail 
3. **Feature faces with expressive emotions** to improve relatability and clickability. 
- Effort: Low 
- Timeline: 30 minutes per thumbnail 
#### Expected Impact: 
- Metric: CTR 
- Current Value: 5.73% 
- Projected Value: 6.5% 
- Percentage Improvement: ~13% 
- Timeframe: 60 days 
- Confidence Level: Medium 
#### Reasoning: Visually appealing thumbnails can significantly increase CTR, as they are the first point of contact with potential viewers. 
#### Success Metrics: 
- Monitor CTR for videos with new thumbnails versus old styles. 
- Evaluate viewer feedback in comments regarding thumbnail preferences. These strategic recommendations are designed to leverage the strengths of "Mulheres em Deus" while addressing critical areas for improvement, aligned with YouTube's 2025 algorithm criteria.
